About

The Identity Film Festival celebrates the ingenuity and creativity of student and alumni filmmakers from around the world. The annual event is held at the Western Academy of Beijing, and not only serves as a showcase of excellent student films, but also as an intimate experience for the student filmmakers to share their experiences.

Submissions close May 6th.

Submission Guidelines

  1. No revisions will be accepted once an entry has been received.
  2. All film genres accepted (documentary, dramas, comedies, experimental, etc.).
  3. The film must be suitable for a general audience.
  4. The duration of the film can be anywhere between 1 and 8 minutes long.
  5. Submissions in other than English must be subtitled in English.
  6. The film must not violate common copyright regulations.

How to Submit

  1. Complete the submission form before submitting your film.
  2. All submission forms must be sent to identityfilmfestival18@gmail.com
  3. An invite-only link for the drive and a contestant number will be sent to the email listed on the submission form once the form is received.
  4. Please name the file as (AssignedNumber.LastName.Title).
    • The file must either be in the mov (QuickTime) or mp4 format.
    • The film must be at least 720p.
